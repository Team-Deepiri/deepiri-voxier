## Optional CC0 texture packs (future)

When adding PNG parallax or sky panoramas, prefer:

- [Kenney Background Elements](https://kenney.nl/assets/background-elements) (CC0)
- [OpenGameArt space backgrounds](https://opengameart.org/art-search-advanced?keys=space+background)

Place files under `Voxier/art/backgrounds/<sector>/` and reference from `BackgroundProfile`.
